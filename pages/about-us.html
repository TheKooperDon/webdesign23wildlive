About Us

Our Story

WiLDLiVE begins with a dream of something more. Every day, thousands of people commute to and from their homes, to work, even to their food and water sources. When you stop to think about this, something becomes clear. Animals do the exact same thing! The amount of modern infrastructure that disrupts natural wildlife is growing every day, and a solution is needed.

Here at WiLDLiVE, we believe that there is a way forward that works for both our furry friends and us. On this website, you will find different methods of integrating human-made infrastructure with the natural world.
